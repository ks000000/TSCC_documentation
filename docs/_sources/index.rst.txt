Welcome to TSCC's documentation!
===================================
Welcome to TSCC (Time Series Check and Correct), the package for data quality management. You can determine and improve
the accuracy of your data quality for right-skewed timeseries. This is a comprehensive guide to using our software.

.. image:: ./_static/Overview.jpg
   :alt: Overview of TSCC's functionalities
   :align: center
